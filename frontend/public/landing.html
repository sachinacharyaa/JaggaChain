How It Works

A transparent, multi-step workflow that keeps every stakeholder accountable — recorded permanently on Solana.

01

Citizen Submits

Landowners submit registration requests or transfer applications with land details, supporting documents, and parcel boundaries via the platform.

CITIZEN
02

Officers Review

Government land officers examine documents, cross-check records, and conduct field verification in a structured multi-level approval workflow.

GOVERNMENT
03

On-Chain Record

Upon final approval, ownership is cryptographically recorded on Solana. The immutable transaction becomes the permanent legal proof of ownership.

BLOCKCHAIN
04

Transfer Request

When land changes hands, the current owner initiates a transfer on-chain, triggering the same rigorous government approval process from start to finish.

CITIZEN
05

Dept. Approval

Senior department heads conduct final review, ensuring all steps were followed and the transfer meets all legal requirements before signing off.

GOVERNMENT
06

New Ownership

The new owner receives a verified Solana transaction as permanent proof of land ownership — accessible and verifiable by anyone, anywhere, forever.

Core Features

Built for Nepal,
Powered by Solana

🔗

Immutable Records

Every registration, transfer, and approval is permanently written to Solana's blockchain. No authority can alter or erase ownership history.

🏛️

Multi-Level Government Workflow

Step-by-step approval process mirrors actual government procedures — ward, municipality, district, and department sign-offs all recorded on-chain.

🔍

Public Verifiability

Any citizen can verify land ownership by looking up a parcel ID on the blockchain. Transparency eliminates disputes before they start.

⚡

Near-Zero Transaction Cost

Solana's high throughput and sub-cent fees make blockchain land registration accessible to every Nepali citizen, not just the privileged few.

🗺️

All 77 Districts

Designed to scale across Nepal's administrative geography — from Taplejung to Darchula, every land parcel gets a unique verifiable identity.

📱

Citizen Dashboard

Track your land registration or transfer status in real time. Receive notifications at every approval step. Full visibility, zero guesswork.
